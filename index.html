Ledger/Trezor

Connecting a hardware wallet to TomoMask makes it simple to have the security of cold storage with the convenience of a hot wallet.

Your private keys will always stay in your Ledger. This allows any dapp that works with TomoMask to be used safely and securely with a hardware wallet

Custom Tokens

TomoMask also functions as a wallet for your browser. This allows you to buy, receive and send TOMO and TRC20 compatible tokens through the extension itself.
